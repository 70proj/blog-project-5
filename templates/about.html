About Me

This is what I do.

Hi there! My name is Muhammad A. Farooq, but you can call me Affan. I’m a passionate software engineer and a Computer Science undergraduate at Queen’s University, specializing in Software Design. I thrive on solving challenging problems, from optimizing algorithms to building full-stack applications with technologies like the MERN stack and Flutter. My academic and professional experiences have shaped me into a well-rounded developer who values scalability, efficiency, and innovation.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I have a deep appreciation for philosophy and enjoy delving into books that explore the complexities of human thought and existence. Reading fuels my curiosity and provides fresh perspectives that I often apply to my problem-solving approach. Running is another passion of mine; it’s not just exercise but a mental reset, offering clarity and a sense of accomplishment.

When I’m not immersed in code or a good book, you’ll find me brainstorming new ideas, collaborating on projects, or seeking out challenges that push me to grow. I believe in the importance of balance: pairing technical rigor with personal growth and creativity. If you’d like to connect or discuss ideas, I’d be thrilled to hear from you!
